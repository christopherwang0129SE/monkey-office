# Research: Noise in Open-Plan Offices

## 1. Effect on Deep Focus & Productivity
* **14% Drop:** Cognitive performance is 14% lower in open-plan setups compared to quiet rooms.
* **66% Decrease:** Productivity drops by up to 66% when employees are exposed to intelligible background conversations.
* **32% Satisfaction:** The Leesman Index (800,000+ respondents) shows only 32% of employees are satisfied with office noise levels.

## 2. Interruptions & Well-being
* **2x Wasted Time:** Employees report wasting twice as much time due to noise interruptions in open offices versus private rooms.
* **#1 Distraction:** Irrelevant speech is the primary source of annoyance, severely impairing working memory, text comprehension, and creative thinking.
* **Increased Stress:** Continuous open-office noise (46–58 dB) actively elevates physiological stress (heart rate and skin conductivity).

## 3. Insights for Your Product
* **45 dB Threshold:** Acoustic satisfaction drops sharply when background noise exceeds 45 dB.
* **Speech vs. Volume:** Track sudden conversational peaks rather than just average ambient noise; intelligible speech breaks focus the most.
* **Behavioral Nudge:** Like Birdie, design the monitor to visually signal when noise crosses the 45–50 dB threshold to prompt quieter behavior.
